What are Incoterms®?
Incoterms® 2020 are globally accepted trade rules published by the International Chamber of Commerce (ICC) that define the responsibilities of exporters and importers in international trade transactions.
🌍
Why Do They Matter?
They eliminate ambiguity in global trade by clearly allocating obligations for freight, insurance, customs clearance, and import duty — reducing disputes and enabling seamless cross-border commerce.

What Will You Do?
